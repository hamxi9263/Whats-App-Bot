------- CHAT LOG 054 | Website Chat | Patient: Ayaan Khan -------
Ayaan: Difference between Sapphire FUE and regular FUE?
Sara: Both are the same concept but with one key difference — the instrument used to create recipient channels. Regular FUE uses steel blades while Sapphire FUE uses sapphire crystal blades. Sapphire is harder and sharper so channels are smaller and more precise, there is less tissue trauma, healing is faster, and scab formation is reduced. Sapphire FUE costs PKR 120,000 to 420,000 versus regular FUE at PKR 80,000 to 350,000.
Ayaan: Is Sapphire worth the extra cost?
Sara: For most patients yes. The faster healing and reduced trauma are significant benefits especially if you cannot take much time off work. For very large sessions some surgeons still prefer regular FUE for efficiency. Doctor will advise best option after assessing your specific case.
